About

After a decade in SaaS sales, I realized I left behind what I enjoyed most in school and my younger years: problem-solving, building, and creating.

I wasn't using my brain the way I wanted to, and I envied the people who built and improved the SaaS products I sold. So I quit my sales job, started coursework with Codecademy and The Odin Project, and got a part-time job to pay the bills.

In my personal time I enjoy cycling, climbing, playing golf and tennis, cooking, and writing jokes (and occasionally saying them in front of people).

Focus

I can see myself enjoying the front-end or back-end or both. So I started with a full-stack approach and will refine my focus after finishing Codecademy's full-stack career path.
